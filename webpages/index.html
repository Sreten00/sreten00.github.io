Welcome







Welcome to the World of Music website!



The aim of this website is to provide an online platform to explore the world of music and to promote the wide ranging and diverse musical styles within music departments in schools. This website relates to the GCSE Music “World Music” module. This website will to allow the user to go on a journey through the world of music, following the development of musical styles and the artists that best represent them.







Benefits from this website



Since the website is aiming at young audience (13-21), this web site can help users to develop their taste for music as well as their personalities since they will encounter a lot of folk music, which is based on feelings and experiences which made them famous since they were relatable or touching to lots of people. Also since users will be relatively young and inexperienced with music, the audience will enrich their knowledge of different types and styles of music
